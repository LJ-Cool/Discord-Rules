Discord Server Rules

General Conduct

  • 1. Respect Everyone: Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • 2. No Spamming: Avoid spamming messages, links, or images. Keep the channels clean and on-topic.
  • 3. Keep it Safe for Work (SFW): All content shared must be appropriate for all ages. No NSFW content is allowed.

Communication and Interaction

  • 4. Use Appropriate Channels: Post in the correct channels. Off-topic discussions should be moved to appropriate areas.
  • 5. No Self-Promotion: Self-promotion, advertising other servers, or posting links to personal projects without permission is prohibited.
  • 6. Constructive Criticism: Provide feedback respectfully. Constructive criticism is welcome, but outright negativity or rudeness is not.

Music and Collaboration

  • 7. Respect Intellectual Property: Do not share music or other content that violates copyright laws. Respect the intellectual property of others.
  • 8. Collaboration Requests: Use the designated channel for collaboration requests and music discussions. Be clear and respectful when proposing ideas.

Moderation and Reporting

  • 9. Follow Moderator Instructions: Listen to the moderators and admins. They are here to help maintain order and ensure everyone enjoys the server.
  • 10. Report Issues: If you see any rule-breaking or inappropriate behavior, report it to the moderators. Do not engage or escalate the situation.

Events and Updates

  • 11. Event Participation: Participate in events and activities as you like. Be respectful of those who organize and take part in these events.
  • 12. Stay Updated: Keep an eye on announcements for updates and important information about the server and upcoming music releases.

Additional Guidelines

  • Language: Keep the primary communication in the server in the designated language (e.g., English), unless specified otherwise in certain channels.
  • Bots and Commands: Use bot commands only in bot-specific channels to avoid cluttering the main chat areas.
  • Voice Channels: Use push-to-talk if you are in a noisy environment. Be considerate of others in voice chats.

Enforcing the Rules

  • Warnings and Bans: Members who break the rules will receive warnings. Continuous rule-breaking will result in temporary or permanent bans.
  • Appeals: If you believe you were wrongfully banned or warned, contact a moderator to discuss the situation respectfully.
